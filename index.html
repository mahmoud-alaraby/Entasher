Small details that dramatically improve the proposals you receive.

1 Scope State the goal, not just the service

Example: "Generate qualified leads in Riyadh" beats "Need marketing."

2 Timing Add a timeline (even if flexible)

When providers know your deadline, they price and plan more accurately.

3 Budget A range is enough

You don't need an exact number ‚Äî a range prevents unrealistic proposals.

4 References Share one example you like

A link or screenshot aligns expectations faster than paragraphs.

5 Decision Say what "good" looks like

KPIs, quality expectations, locations, deliverables ‚Äî keep it simple.
